This parish is one of the many parishes in Ireland benefiting from the Parish Cells experience. Why not visit a Parish Cell this year? You will be glad you did!

Parish cell groups meet in a number of parishioners homes on a fortnightly basis, usually on a Wednesday night at 8pm.
