Mission Quotes

"No Christian community is faithful to its mission unless it is missionary. Either it is a missionary community or it is not even a Christian community"
(John Paul II Redempt. Miss.)


"The entire mission of the Church is concentrated and manifested in Evangelisation."
(John Paul II, Christifideles Laici)


"The Church exists to evangelise."
(Paul VI, Evang. Nunc.)


"The Church becomes at one and the same time an Evangelising and Evangelised community"
(John Paul II, Christifideles Laici)


"Communion gives rise to Mission and Mission is fufilled in Communion"
(John Paul II, Christifideles Laici)
