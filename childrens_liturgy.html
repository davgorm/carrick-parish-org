Children's Liturgy

At the Sunday Masses in Carrickfergus and Whitehead children between the ages 4-11 are invited to join the Children's Liturgy Group during the early part of Mass and will be helped to understand the Gospel in language they understand. The children process to another room at the beginning of mass and return as part of the offertory procession, at which time they present the message they received from the gospel. A message from the children:


"Jesus loves you".
